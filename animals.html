Meet the Residents

Our Animals

Every animal at Cookie's Cuddle Farm has a unique story. Through rehabilitation, compassionate care, and plenty of love, each one has found a safe, forever home. Meet the incredible personalities who make our farm so special.

Animal Highlights

Meet the Residents

Duke

Duke was originally raised as a 4-H project before being returned to his breeder. From the moment we met him, his affectionate personality and gentle charm stole our hearts. We were thrilled to welcome him to Cookie's Cuddle Farm, where he'll spend the rest of his life surrounded by love.

Juancho

Juancho is a hilarious duck with a big personality and a touch of "Napoleon syndrome." When an unexpected move meant his family could no longer keep him and his bonded mate, Patty, they entrusted them to our care. Juancho now enjoys life as the only drake among his flock of ladies, soaking up attention from visitors and staff alike.

Kevin

Kevin is one of the few animals we intentionally raised from a chick because of his exceptional temperament. As the head rooster, he's often the first to greet guests in the chicken yard. Protective of his hens yet incredibly affectionate, Kevin is happiest when he's keeping watch — or enjoying a cuddle.

Lucky & Fortune

Lucky and his companion, Fortune, were on their way to processing when we received the call that changed their lives. We welcomed them to the farm, where they've become entertaining, gentle companions and close friends to our donkeys.

The Ladies

When a breeder who deeply loved her donkeys could no longer care for them due to health challenges, she entrusted them to us. We are incredibly grateful to provide these wonderful girls with a forever home. Each has her own unique personality, but Cookie's calm nature and diva-like charm inspired the farm's name. Pictured here are Cookie and Daisy enjoying a walk with two of our wonderful staff members.

Yoyo & Bobby

Yoyo and Bobby were bottle-fed after their mother sadly passed away on a meat farm. Raising them created an incredible bond between the lambs and our staff from the very beginning. Today, these sweet boys are gentle, affectionate cuddlers who have a special way of connecting with calm, kind-hearted visitors.

Oliver

Oliver was dearly loved by his previous family, who made the difficult decision to rehome him when they could no longer meet his needs. After a visit to the farm, he settled into our pack with ease. Intelligent, playful, and always ready for cuddles, Oliver continues to enjoy visits from his former family, with whom we proudly maintain a close relationship.

Princess Penelope

Princess Penelope, affectionately known as Penny, came to us from a suburban family who wanted the best for her but no longer had the time to meet her needs. We were delighted to welcome this adorable mini pig to the farm. Penny is every bit the diva — she loves belly rubs, tasty treats, and making sure she's the center of attention alongside her farm friends.
