Every animal at Cookie's Cuddle Farm has been rescued, rehabilitated, and given a forever home. Get to know the amazing personalities who live here.

Forever Residents

Who Lives Here

From playful goats to gentle pigs, our residents each have unique personalities and stories that will capture your heart.

Goats

Our mischievous, playful goats love attention and won't hesitate to investigate your pockets for treats. Each one has been rescued from difficult circumstances and now lives their best life here at the farm.

Pigs

Incredibly intelligent and full of personality, our pigs love belly rubs and rooting around in fresh soil. They remind us every day just how sensitive and social farm animals truly are.

Chickens & Birds

Our feathered residents range from bold to shy, but all enjoy free-range days in the sunshine. Many were rescued from factory farming situations and have blossomed into their true selves.

Rabbits & Small Animals

Gentle and curious, our small animal residents love quiet moments and gentle hands. Each one has a unique personality that visitors can't help but fall in love with.

Donkeys

Sweet, calm, and deeply affectionate, our donkeys are some of the most beloved residents on the farm. They form strong bonds with people and other animals alike.

Sheep

Often misunderstood, our sheep are actually quite social and smart. They recognize faces and form lasting friendships — with each other and with the people who care for them.
