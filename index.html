Our Story

A Place Built on Love

Cookie's Cuddle Farm and Sanctuary was founded with one simple belief: every animal deserves a life free from fear and suffering. What started as one person's passion grew into a thriving sanctuary where rescued farm animals receive the care, space, and love they need to thrive.

From goats and pigs to chickens and rabbits, our animals each have a unique story — and a permanent home with us. We are committed to education, community connection, and the highest standard of animal welfare.

Meet the Residents

Our Animals

Every resident at Cookie's Cuddle Farm has been rescued, rehabilitated, and given a forever home. Get to know a few of our beloved animals below.

Goats

Our mischievous, playful goats love attention and won't hesitate to investigate your pockets for treats. Each one has rescued from difficult circumstances and now lives their best life.

Pigs

Incredibly intelligent and full of personality, our pigs love belly rubs and rooting around in fresh soil. They remind us every day just how sensitive and social farm animals truly are.

Chickens & Birds

Our feathered residents range from bold to shy, but all enjoy free-range days in the sunshine. Many were rescued from factory farming situations and have blossomed into their true selves.

Rabbits & Small Animals

Gentle and curious, our small animal residents love quiet moments and gentle hands. Each one has a unique personality that visitors can't help but fall in love with.
