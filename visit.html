Plan Your Visit

We love welcoming guests to the farm! Whether you're coming on a group tour, with family, or on your own, a visit to Cookie's Cuddle Farm is an experience you won't forget.

What to Expect

Your Farm Visit

When you visit Cookie's Cuddle Farm, you'll have the chance to meet our rescued animals up close, learn about their stories, and experience the joy of connecting with nature and animals in a peaceful, loving environment.

  • Hours:By appointment — contact us to schedule your visit
  • Location:Address provided upon booking confirmation
  • Duration:Visits typically last 1–3 hours
  • Groups:Specializing in senior center and hospital visits. Additional groups available upon request.
  • Accessibility:We strive to make the farm accessible for everyone — please contact us with any specific needs ahead of your visit
  • What to wear:Comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes — it's a real working farm!
