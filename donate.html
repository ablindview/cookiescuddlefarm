Your Impact

Where Your Money Goes

100% of donations go directly to the animals in our care. Here's how your generosity makes a difference:

🌾

Food & Nutrition

Feeding dozens of animals every day adds up. Your support keeps our residents healthy and well-fed year round.

🏠

Shelter & Housing

Safe, comfortable, and clean living spaces for all of our animals — from goat enclosures to chicken coops.

🩺

Veterinary Care

Regular checkups, emergency treatments, and medications for animals who often arrive with health challenges.

🔧

Farm Maintenance

Keeping the farm safe, clean, and functional takes ongoing investment — your help makes it possible.
