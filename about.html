Our Story

About Cookie's Cuddle Farm

Cookie's Cuddle Farm & Rescue was founded on a simple but powerful belief: when animals are given love, safety, and compassion, they often return those gifts in extraordinary ways. Our mission is built on the idea that healing is mutual—rescued animals find hope, and in turn, they help people heal.

How It Started

A Place Built on Love

Cookie's Cuddle Farm and Rescue was founded with one simple belief: every animal deserves a life free from fear and suffering. What started as one person's passion for animals grew into a thriving sanctuary where rescued farm animals receive the care, space, and love they need to thrive.

Our founder Cookie saw firsthand the struggles that farm animals face — from overcrowded conditions to neglect and abandonment. Determined to make a difference, she opened the gates of the farm and hasn't looked back since.

Today, Cookie's Cuddle Farm is home to dozens of animals, each with their own story, personality, and spirit. We are committed to education, community connection, and the highest standard of animal welfare.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Everything we do at Cookie's Cuddle Farm is guided by a set of core values that put animals first.

🐾

Rescue & Rehabilitation

We take in animals from difficult or dangerous situations and give them the medical care, space, and time they need to recover and thrive.

💜

Compassionate Care

Every animal in our care is treated with the same kindness and respect we would give any family member. No animal is "just" anything here.

📚

Education & Awareness

We believe knowledge creates change. Through farm visits, community events, and outreach, we help people understand and connect with farm animals.

🤝

Community Connection

We are stronger together. From our volunteers to our donors and visitors, our community is the heartbeat of everything we do.
