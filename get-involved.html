Volunteering

Join Our Volunteer Team

Our volunteers are the backbone of Cookie's Cuddle Farm. Without their time and dedication, we simply couldn't do what we do. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or a whole weekend, we'd love to have you on board.

Volunteer opportunities include:

  • Daily animal feeding and care
  • Cleaning and maintaining animal enclosures
  • Assisting with farm tours and visitor experiences
  • Help with events and fundraisers
  • Photography and social media support
  • Administrative and office assistance
